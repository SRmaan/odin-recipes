Roti

What is Roti?

Roti is a flatbread that is native to India. Roti is also referred to as Chapati by other Indian states. To read more about this click here!

Ingredients

  • Atta (flour)
  • Water
  • Rolling pin
  • Skillet

How do you make it?

  1. 2 cups atta whole wheat flour, 270 grams + 1/4 cup for rolling the roti
  2. 1-2 teaspoons oil optional, 5-10 ml.
  3. water as needed to knead a soft dough, I used around 3/4 cup (180 ml) + 1 tablespoon (15 ml)
  4. ghee to brush the rotis.

Now that we got that out the way, here comes the fun part!

  1. Preheat your Skillet
  2. Roll out the dough with the Rolling Pin until a circular shape is formed
  3. Put your dough onto the Skillet and flip dough every 30~ sec until thoroughly cooked
  4. Pick up your finished roti and place on plate to cool
