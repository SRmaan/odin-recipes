Kheer

What is Kheer?

Kheer is a sweet Indian rice pudding dish. To read more about this click here!

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup rice 50 grams, I used basmati rice
  • 1 teaspoon ghee also known as clarified butter
  • 3-4 green cardamom pods slightly crushed
  • 1 liter whole milk
  • 4-5 tablespoons sugar 50-62.5 grams, adjust to taste
  • 3 tablespoons chopped nuts I used chopped cashews & almonds
  • 1.5 teaspoons rose water (optional)

How do you make it?

  1. Rinse the rice until water turns clear. Soak the rice in enough water for 20 to 30 minutes. Drain the rice and set aside
  2. Place a pan on medium heat. Add 1 teaspoon of ghee to it and add rice. Add 3-4 crushed green cardamom pods
  3. Toss the rice with the ghee and cardamom for 1 to 2 minutes, stirring constantly until aromatic
  4. Add the milk to the pan and stir well. Set heat to medium-high.
  5. Let the milk come to a boil. Stir in between so that milk doesn't stuck to the bottom of the pan.
  6. Once the milk has come to a boil, lower the heat to low and let the kheer cook for around 25 minutes on low heat. Stir every 2 minutes or so. The milk will reduce considerably after 25 minutes and the kheer would look thick and the rice will be completely cooked.
  7. Add in the sugar and mix. Also add in the nuts.
  8. Remove pan after 5 min. from heat. Garnish with more nuts and serve kheer warm or chilled.

Complimented after a nice hearty meal!
