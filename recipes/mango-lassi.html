Mango Lassi

What is Lassi?

A lassi is a traditional Indian drink which is similar to a fruit smoothie. To read more about this click here!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup chopped very ripe mango (see how to peel and chop mango), frozen chopped mango, or canned mango pulp
  • 1 cup plain yogurt
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 4 teaspoons honey or sugar, more or less to taste
  • Dash ground cardamom (optional)
  • Ice (optional)

How do you make it?

  1. Put the mango, yogurt, milk, honey (or sugar), and cardamom into a blender and blend for 2 minutes
  2. Pour contents into a glass and sprinkle with a tiny pinch of ground cardamom to serve

Enjoy on a nice hot summer day!
